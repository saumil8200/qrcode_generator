What is a QR Code?

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) is a versatile and high-capacity two-dimensional barcode that can store a wealth of information. Unlike traditional barcodes, QR codes can be scanned from any angle and hold more data, making them an ideal tool for a wide range of applications.

Why Use QR Codes?

Instant Access

QR codes can link to websites, social media profiles, contact information, and more. Simply scan the code with your smartphone, and you’re instantly connected.

Versatile Data Storage

Whether you want to share a URL, a text message, or even a Wi-Fi network login, QR codes can handle it all.

Marketing and Branding

Enhance your marketing campaigns with custom QR codes that lead to promotions, special offers, or your brand’s online presence.

Convenience

QR codes streamline processes by eliminating the need for manual entry. They are widely used in payments, ticketing, and inventory management.
